Redefining Dark Tourism

An innovative Location-Based AR application transforming passive observation into active participation through spatial computing, empathic narrative, and survival mechanics.

AR & 3D Visual Design Spatial Computing UI/UX

The Problem / 问题

Originally conceptualized to solve the issue of disrespectful "influencer" behavior at the Chernobyl site. Traditional tour guides suffered from overcrowding, bad guidance, and a failure to convey the gravity of the history, leading to tourists prioritizing social media over historical grief.

The Solution / 解决方案

Evolving the tour into an immersive spatial narrative. By integrating an empathic AR Navigation System, an engaging Puzzle System, and an adrenaline-pumping AR Battle Royale Game, users don't just see history; they navigate its memories and fight to rewrite its tragic climax.
